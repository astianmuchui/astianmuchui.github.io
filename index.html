About

A Problem Solver at the Crossroads of Software & Hardware

I am a self-taught engineer with a vast array of experiences and skills that enable me to approach different types of problems and work in multiple settings. Currently, I am an undergraduate Control and Instrumentation student at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology and a certified software engineer through the ALX program.

As a software engineer, I focus on the solution and its sustainability. My programming background spans web applications, distributed systems, IoT, and robotics. I have substantial experience working with and setting up Linux systems, building multi-protocol communication platforms, and architecting backend services in Go, Node.js, and Python.

As a student, my aim is to learn as much as possible and apply it in the real world for research and to come up with innovative ways to solve problems.
