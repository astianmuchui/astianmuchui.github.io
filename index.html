About Me

A Problem
Solver in
Software
& Hardware

I am a self-taught engineer with a broad array of experiences that enable me to approach different types of problems and work in multiple settings. Currently an undergraduate Control and Instrumentation student at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology, and a certified software engineer through the ALX program.

As a software engineer, I focus on the solution and its sustainability. My programming background spans web applications, distributed systems, IoT, and robotics. I have substantial experience working with Linux systems, building multi-protocol communication platforms, and architecting backend services in Go, Node.js, and Python.

As a student, my aim is to learn as much as possible and apply it in the real world for research and to come up with innovative ways to solve problems.
