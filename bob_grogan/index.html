About Us

At Bob Grogan, our focus is evidence-based strategy and innovation. We use data science and business intelligence to provide our clients with insights to develop business strategies that work. We have a pool of Business Analysts specialized in corporate governance, ICT, HR, Audit and Finance, Marketing, Risk, Supply Chain and ISO Management Systems. Our Technical team has wide experience supporting SMEs in clean energy, WASH, health, agribusiness, education, hospitality and value addition. We offer training and consultancy in policy and strategy for public and private sectors.
